About This Project

I developed a robust Spring Boot application designed for comprehensive blog management, featuring a range of functionalities to enhance both user and admin experiences. The application supports the creation, uploading, editing, and deletion of blog posts, with a secure authentication system requiring users to log in or sign up to access these features.

For user management, the application includes encrypted password storage to ensure user privacy and security. Users can create and manage their own blog posts, including editing content and updating images. However, they are restricted from altering the author name of their posts, which is reserved for admin users only.

Admins possess extended privileges within the system. They can create, edit, or delete any blog posts, including those of other users, and modify associated images. Additionally, admins have the authority to change the author names on posts. The admin interface also includes functionalities to view and manage user information, such as the total number of users and detailed profiles, although sensitive details like passwords remain inaccessible due to encryption, maintaining user privacy.

The application utilizes HTML and JSP to deliver a dynamic, user-friendly interface, while SQL is employed for reliable data management and storage. This project not only demonstrates my proficiency in developing full-stack applications but also highlights my ability to implement secure, scalable, and user-centric features within a blog management system.

Login Button: Access your personalized dashboard, manage your blog posts, and engage with the community by logging into your account.

Clicking on the Login button will direct you to our secure login page. Here, you will be prompted to enter your email address and password to access your account.

The Login page allows you to access your account by entering your registered email address and password. If the credentials you provide are incorrect, you will see an “Invalid email address or password” message. For users who have forgotten their password, there is a Forgot Password? link to initiate a password reset. New users can click on the Register button to sign up for an account.

For new users, the Register button on the login page allows you to easily sign up and create a new account. Simply click the button and provide your Name, Email address, Password, and Phone number. Once you've entered these details, you'll join our community and gain access to all the features of our blog management system.

If you’ve forgotten your password, don’t worry! Simply click on the Forgot Password? link, and follow the easy steps to reset it. You’ll need to provide a valid email address and phone number for verification. After entering and confirming your new password, you'll receive a success message if the email and phone number are validated. You will then be redirected to the login page, where you can log in with your new password.

Once you log in, you will be redirected to the Blogs page, where you'll see a warm Welcome message along with your name. From this page, you can start creating new blogs by clicking on the Create Blog button. You can also manage your existing content through the My Blog button, which allows you to edit your own posts. Additionally, use the Search bar to find specific blogs or explore various topics.

When you click on the Create Blog button, you'll be prompted to log in if you aren't already. If you are logged in, you will be redirected to the Create Blog page. Here, you can optionally upload an image file for your blog, and then enter the blog title and blog content. As a user, your name will automatically be set as the author name and cannot be changed. Once you submit your blog, it will be uploaded successfully, and you'll be redirected back to the Blogs page with a message stating “New Blog Added”.

After creating a blog, you will see a "New Blog Added" message, and your blog will appear at the top of the Blogs page. Your name will be displayed as the author name, and you’ll see that the title and image (if uploaded) are perfectly displayed.

You can manage your own blogs by clicking on the My Blog button. This section displays a list of your blogs, each with Edit and Delete icons. Use these icons to update or remove your blogs as needed.

By clicking the Edit icon on your blog, you will be taken to the update page. Here, you can view your existing blog details, including the previously uploaded image displayed on the side. You have the option to change the image, or if you prefer to remove it, select the “Yes” option to delete it. You can also update the title and content as desired. Note that as a logged-in user, you cannot change the author name; it will always be set to your name automatically.

After updating your blog, you will be redirected to the My Blog section, where you'll see a "Blog Updated" message confirming that your changes have been saved. You can review the updated details, including any changes to the title, content, and image. The author name will remain as your name, ensuring consistency.

In the My Blog section, you can delete your own blog by clicking the Delete icon next to the blog you wish to remove. A pop-up alert will appear asking, "Are you sure you want to delete [Your Blog Name]?" You will have the option to Confirm if you wish to proceed with the deletion or Cancel if you decide not to delete the blog.

After deleting a blog, you will be redirected to the My Blog section, where you will see a "Blog Deleted" message confirming that the blog has been removed. If there are no remaining blogs in this section, you will be prompted with a message saying, "You have no blogs. Create a Blog." This invites you to create a new blog to continue contributing content.

When an admin logs in, they will be welcomed with a "Welcome Admin" message on the Blogs section. Additionally, a special Check Users button will be visible, which is accessible only to the admin. This button allows the admin to manage user accounts.

As an admin, you have full access to the system, including the ability to edit or delete any blog across the platform. This ensures that the admin can oversee and manage all content effectively.

When the admin clicks the Edit icon on any blog in the Blogs section, they can modify the title, content, and image of the blog. Unlike regular users, the admin also has the ability to change the author name. Additionally, the admin can delete any blog, including those of specific users. This feature is crucial for removing any content that is deemed inappropriate or violates the platform's guidelines.

When the admin clicks the Check Users button in the Blogs section, they can view detailed information for all users, including their name, email, and phone number. However, the admin cannot see user passwords, as these are kept private and encrypted to ensure user security.

If necessary, the admin can delete a specific user from the list. Clicking the Delete button will trigger a confirmation pop-up alert, asking, "Are you sure you want to delete this user?" After clicking Confirm, the user will be permanently removed from the list. Once deleted, the user will no longer have login credentials and will need to register or sign up for a new account to access the platform again.
